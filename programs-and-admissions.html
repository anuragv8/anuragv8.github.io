ADMISSION PROCESS

ENQUIRE

Call or email us and set up a time to meet with us. We will take you around the school for a tour of the campus.

OBSERVATION

After the tour, we recommend that you fix a time to observe the classroom and children in session. The Montessori Method has to be seen in order to believe.

APPLICATION

Discuss with us your queries and concerns and Collect an application form from the office. Submit the filled in application along with 4 Passport photos of the child and a copy of the birth certificate.

MEET THE TEACHER

Meet the concerned teacher with your child. This helps the child to have someone they have met and know at school in order to settle better.
